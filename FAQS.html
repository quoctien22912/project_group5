Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you export ?

We export to the global importers,wholesalers and retailers.

Can you deliver anywhere in the world ?

Yes, we deliver worldwide to the global importers,wholesalers and retailers.

Do product prices vary from country to country?

The product price remains same irrespective of the country you shop from. Only the shipping prices change depending upon your delivery location.

Who can create an account and place an order on you website?

Anyone who is of 18 years old and above will be allowed to create an account and place an order online.

What are the currency available for shopping on the website?

You can shop on the website in VND .

How much time will it take for my product to get delivered?

All shipments are delivered in 7-10 business days once the order is received by us.
